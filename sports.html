FIFA World Cup U-17 2023


Main Idea: The Country Selection of World Cup U-17
Main Point: FIFA World Cup U-17 will be held in Indonesia from 10 November 2023 — 2 December 2023. Indonesia had a first time became the host of FIFA World Cup U-17. Due to COVID-19 in 2021, The World Cup canceled until 2 years from 2021. They gonna started on 2023. Before Indonesia, There's another country was selected. It was Peru. Peru withdrew on 3 April 2023. On 23 June 2023, FIFA officially appointed Indonesia as the new host, believed to have been in compensation for the loss of the 2023 FIFA U-20 World Cup hosting rights which were given to Argentina after Indonesia took issue with the Israeli team in that tournament
