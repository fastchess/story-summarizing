Morbius


In Greece, young Michael and his friend Milo share a blood illness and dream of being normal. Michael is sent to New York for medical school while Milo is cared for by their adoptive father, Nicholas. After killing his crew in a test, he contacts authorities and jumps overboard. Back in New York, he discovers his new vampire abilities and that his artificial blood no longer satisfies his hunger. Michael is arrested but escapes, develops a cure, and confronts Milo. Milo wounds their adoptive father and kills Martine, a colleague. Michael drinks Martine's blood, gains power from bats, defeats Milo with a cure, and mourns his losses.
